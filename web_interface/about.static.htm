About

One of the most important and memorable part of a college students life is the enrollment process. Some students love it some hate it, but most students hate it. We think the most hard and pain staking part of the whole enrollment process is the dreaded plotting of ones schedule. We’e all been there, being able to plot the perfect schedule only to be told when you are all ready to print your study load that one or several of the subjects in your schedule are already closed or the schedule does not exists then you have to re do all your scheduling. These kinds of situations are really annoying and sometimes heart breaking.

We, the SKEMO team thought these kinds of situations are really troublesome to the college student’ already stressful life and frankly unnecessary! So we decided to develop a system that is able to do this vital part of the enrollment process which is mainly:


  • Let the student know what subjects are allowed for him or her to take on that semester.
  • Let the student choose from a list of subjects for him or her to enroll without being concern of the schedule.
  • Give students the freshest information about the schedules of the subjects being offered on that semester.
  • And finally why not try to let the system plot there schedules for them.

We think that these four functionality is very important in coming up with a system that can help ease the enrollment nightmare. Because of the need for this functionality SKEMO, The Subject Schedule Recommender was born. SKEMO or “kedul mo”is an application with one thing in mind and that is to lessen the pain of plotting your schedule upon enrollment. SKEMO is design to do all the four functionalities stated above and even a bit more. Never before has a system like this been implemented in USJ-R. We truly hope that this system would help our fellow josenians in their college life.
